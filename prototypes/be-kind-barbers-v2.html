Marcus Bell

Marcus has spent over a decade behind the chair — from Cuba Street barbershops to a room he can finally call his own. Skin fades, scissor work, a straight-razor finish: he treats every cut like it’s the only one that day.

He reckons a good cut starts with a good conversation, and that nobody should walk into a barbershop feeling like they don’t belong. Sit down, tell him how your week’s been, and walk out looking — and feeling — sharper.
